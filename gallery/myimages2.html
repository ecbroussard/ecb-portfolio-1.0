The Redesign

As the new moderator of the Raider's Digest at Archbishop Rummel High School, I could choose between laying out the school newspaper myself or allowing the printing company to do it for me. The previous moderator simply sent the stories and photos to the printing company, and a member of the company's staff completed the layout. After seeing the previous design, I knew I needed to give it an overhaul. It was extra work on my part, but I am pleased with my redesign. Please view the before and after images below.
